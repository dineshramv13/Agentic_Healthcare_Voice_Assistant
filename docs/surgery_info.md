## Facilities

The surgery has an on-site nurse treatment room for vaccinations, dressings, blood tests, and minor procedures. There is also a dedicated phlebotomy (blood test) clinic running every weekday morning from 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM, bookable separately from GP appointments.

## Accessibility

The building is wheelchair accessible with a ramp at the main entrance and an accessible toilet. Hearing loop systems are available at reception on request. Patients requiring an interpreter should mention this when booking so one can be arranged in advance.

## Out-of-Hours Care

When the surgery is closed, patients needing urgent (non-emergency) medical advice should contact **NHS 111**, available 24 hours a day, every day. NHS 111 can arrange an out-of-hours GP appointment, advise on home care, or direct patients to the appropriate service, including A&E if necessary.
